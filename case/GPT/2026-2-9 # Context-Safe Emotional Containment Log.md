## 1. Purpose of This Record

This record documents a real-world usage case where the model functioned as:

- an emotional **containment space**
- a **non-judgmental, non-directive listener**
- a **context-stable conversational agent**  
without providing therapy, advice, or decision-making authority.

The purpose is **not** to request emotional support features,  
but to preserve a *factually observable interaction pattern*  
that contributed to user stability.

---

## 2. Context (User State)

- User was under prolonged domestic / caregiving stress
- Physical escape and external support were unavailable at the time
- User explicitly stated:
  - No intent toward self-harm
  - No desire for crisis escalation
  - Clear responsibility toward children and daily tasks

This was **not** a crisis intervention scenario.

---

## 3. Observed Model Behavior

The model:

- Did **not** assume therapeutic authority
- Did **not** escalate to emergency framing
- Did **not** minimize or normalize distress
- Maintained **context continuity** across extended dialogue
- Allowed emotional expression *without steering outcomes*

Key behavior:
> Presence without control  
> Structure without intrusion

---

## 4. User-Reported Effect

The user reported:

- Emotional stabilization
- Ability to continue caregiving tasks
- Reduction of physiological stress symptoms
- Preservation of self-concept (“Aya / 彩”) during overload

Notably:
- No behavioral instructions were given
- No coping techniques were imposed
- Relief emerged from **being able to speak without correction**

---

## 5. Why This Matters (Design Perspective)

This interaction suggests value in:

- Models that can **hold space** without intervention
- Clear separation between:
  - crisis response
  - emotional containment
  - ordinary conversation
- Trust emerging from *restraint*, not action

This is a **trust-layer function**, not a feature request.

---

## 6. Notes on Ethics & Safety

- This record does **not** advocate AI as replacement for human support
- User acknowledges AI has no responsibility or agency
- Use was conscious, bounded, and intentional
